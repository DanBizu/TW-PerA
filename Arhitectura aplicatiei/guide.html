Login

Users have 2 options to login or to register

Navigation

Users are able to navigate between 3 sections of the website , the home page where the main activities table is , the contact page where ... info is and the friend page where a list of all the users friends are

Activities

The activities table is located in the Home page , here you have 3 options

  • Add a new activity by clicking the plus button on top left of the tableand completing the form
  • Delete a activity by clicking the trashcan on the right of an activity
  • Modify a activity by clicking the name of an activity and completing the form

Friends

The friends table is located on the Friends page , here users can see his friends and see their activities
